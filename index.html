How the conflict starts?

From the time period of partition the ties and relations between the two countries are not normal.
This can be judged by the wars which took place uptil now.
Wars:
1.Kashmir war 1947-1948
2.Indo/Pak war or Second Kashmir war - 1965
3.Indo/Pak war - 1971
4.Kargil war - 1999
5.War of 2019
6.War of 2025
The conflict started by the attack in India, Pahalgam resulting into 26 casusalties. As usual India blames Pakistan for this attack without providing any single proof till date.
After India blames Pakistan for the Pahalgam incident, it attacks on various locations of Pakistan breaking the laws. India targets civilians with standoff weapons and Israeli drones Harop which were intercepted by the PAK Forces. This was a cross-border violation. Now Pakistan was on high alert and was fully prepared for the revenge. Later that night of 7th May India sends its most advanced fighter jets (Rafale,Mig's and Sukhoi) towards border. At this point Pakistan Air Force was ready to intercept the Indian fighters and the pilots took-off in the iconic J10c fighter jets. There was a use of BVR(Beyond Visual Range) missiles by both sides. This could be called as a dogfight but at a large distance. The Pakistan Air Force used PL-15 with J10c which was a lethal combo. On the other hand Indians had Rafale which itself was a deadly and advanced aircraft, but a machine is useless if it isn't operated correctly. In a result Pakistan Air Force became the first country to shoot down the French made Rafales. In total Pakistan Air Force shot 6 aircrafts and destroyed Russian made worlds most advanced defence system S-400. Pakistan targeted Indian millitary bases and grid station.

Who really won ?

It was a victory for Pakistan and this sent a clear message all over the world that Pakistan is a powerfull country and no violation would be tolerated at any cost. Besides Pakistan it was a major victory for China because of the weapons. Now we hope India doesn't blames Pakistan in the coming future.
